Tourist attractions

CN Tower

One of the most popular tourist attractions in Toronto is the CN Tower. This iconic structure is one of the tallest freestanding towers in the world and offers breathtaking views of the city from its observation deck and glass floor. Visitors can also enjoy a meal at the tower's revolving restaurant, 360 The Restaurant.

Toronto islands

For those interested in nature, the Toronto Islands are a must-visit. This chain of islands is located just off the coast of the city and is a popular destination for picnics, hiking, and beach activities. Visitors can take a ferry from the mainland to the islands and enjoy a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of the city.

Royal Toronto Museum

Another must-visit attraction in Toronto is the Royal Ontario Museum, which is one of the largest museums in North America. The museum has a vast collection of art, culture, and natural history, showcasing the rich history and diversity of Canada. Visitors can explore galleries that feature everything from dinosaurs to contemporary art.

Art gallary

The Art Gallery of Ontario is another popular cultural attraction in Toronto. The gallery features an extensive collection of Canadian and international art, including works by some of the world's most renowned artists. Visitors can explore exhibitions, attend lectures, and participate in workshops.
