Facilities

Accommodation

The hotel has 97 rooms and suites, each featuring a unique design and luxury amenities such as a flat-screen TV, an espresso machine, and a private bathroom.

Dining

The hotel features two dining options, the Café Boulud and the One Restaurant. Café Boulud is a French-style brasserie and One Restaurant offers contemporary cuisine.

Spa and wellness

The hotel's Spa at The Hazelton offers a range of treatments, including massages, facials, and body treatments. The spa also features a sauna and steam room.

Fitness center

The hotel has a state-of-the-art fitness center with cardio equipment, strength-training machines, and free weights.

Meeting and event spaces

The hotel has several meeting and event spaces, including a ballroom and several private dining rooms.
