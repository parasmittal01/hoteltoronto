Food speaciality

Toronto is known for its diverse and eclectic food scene, offering a wide range of cuisine options to suit all tastes and budgets. Here are some of the city's most popular and unique food specialties:

Poutine

A classic Canadian dish, poutine consists of French fries topped with cheese curds and gravy. This delicious and comforting dish is widely available throughout Toronto, and there are many variations and styles to choose from.

Smoked Meat

A staple of Montreal-style delis, smoked meat is a salty and savory beef brisket that is slow-smoked and served on rye bread with mustard. Toronto is home to many excellent delis that serve this classic dish.

Dim Sum

Toronto has a large Chinese population, and as a result, there are many excellent Chinese restaurants serving a variety of traditional dishes, including dim sum. This popular cuisine is a type of Cantonese food that features small, bite-sized portions of savory and sweet dishes, served in steamer baskets.

Cattibbean Cuisine

Toronto is home to a large Caribbean community, and as a result, there are many excellent Caribbean restaurants serving up authentic and flavorful dishes such as jerk chicken, roti, and goat stew.

Pizza

Toronto has a thriving pizza scene, with many excellent pizzerias offering a range of styles, from traditional Neapolitan to New York-style and beyond. Whether you prefer a thin-crust Margherita or a thick-crust pepperoni, you're sure to find a delicious pizza in Toronto.

Craft Beer

Craft Beer: Toronto has a growing craft beer scene, with many local breweries and taprooms offering a range of unique and flavorful beers. From hoppy IPAs to rich stouts, there is a beer to suit every taste.
